Software selection

Heat Load Software Comparison for HVAC Teams

Which software fits which heat load workflow? This overview groups specialist tools, energy platforms, online reports and Prometo as a PDF and takeoff workflow.

Short answer

If calculation depth is the priority, start with specialist software. If PV, heat pumps, offers and project processes matter, evaluate energy platforms. If the project starts with PDF floor plans and room data, Prometo is an early-stage beta workflow to consider.

Reviewed by Team Prometo · Last updated: June 17, 2026

Specialist tools

Hottgenroth, Dendrit, Linear and Solar-Computer for deep building services calculations.

Energy platforms

autarc and Reonic for broader PV, heat pump, offer and project workflows.

Online reports

Heizreport for guided online heat load reporting workflows.

PDF and takeoff

Prometo for PDF floor plans, room data, digital takeoff and preparation.

FAQ

Questions about heat load software selection

Which type should I compare first?

Start with the workflow: full calculation, broad energy project, quick online report, scan workflow or PDF plan preparation.

When does Prometo fit?

When existing PDF floor plans, room data and takeoff need to become a cleaner basis for later heat load work.

Does this replace professional checks?

No. Every software choice still needs project-specific technical review and responsible use.
